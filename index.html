Syllabus Review for Writing Emphasis (WEM)

Upload your course syllabus to receive feedback on its Writing Emphasis Module (WEM) components. I'll evaluate your syllabus against TCU's four key WEM criteria:

  1. Explicit WEM instruction — Class time dedicated to WEM concepts with clear aims and outcomes
  2. Multiple writing forms — Instruction on various forms with attention to language use and audience
  3. Formative feedback — Student work with instructor feedback throughout the semester
  4. Process & reflection — Focus on writing/revision processes with student awareness
